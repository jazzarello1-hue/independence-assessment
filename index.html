Independence Readiness Assessment

Monthly Check-In for Teenagers (Under 18)

Part 1: Life Skills & Daily Habits

1. How consistently do you maintain personal hygiene (shower, brush teeth, clean clothes)?
2. Do you manage your time well (school work, chores, sleep schedule)?
3. How often do you get adequate sleep (7-9 hours)?
4. Do you exercise or stay physically active?
5. How well do you handle your school/work responsibilities?
6. Can you manage basic first aid (bandages, ice packs, knowing when to ask for help)?
7. Do you eat balanced meals regularly?
8. Can you navigate using maps or GPS independently?
9. Do you limit screen time and avoid excessive social media/gaming?

Part 2: Household Contribution

10. How often do you help cook meals or prep food?
11. Do you clean up after yourself without being asked?
12. How well do you manage your own laundry?
13. Do you keep your personal space clean and organized?
14. How often do you help with household chores (vacuuming, dishes, trash)?
15. Can you do basic household maintenance (change light bulbs, unclog drains, etc.)?
16. Do you help with yard work or outdoor maintenance?
17. Can you grocery shop and make a shopping list?
18. Do you help care for pets (if applicable)?

Part 3: Financial Literacy

19. Do you understand how to create and follow a budget?
20. Do you track your spending and save money regularly?
21. Do you understand how bills work (rent, utilities, insurance)?
22. Can you make smart spending decisions (needs vs. wants)?
23. Do you manage your allowance responsibly (tithe, save, spend wisely)?
24. Do you understand investing basics (stocks, retirement, compound interest)?
25. Can you compare prices and make cost-effective choices?
26. Do you understand credit, debt, and interest?
27. Can you research major purchases and avoid impulse buying?

Part 4: Personal Discipline & Character

28. How well do you control your emotions when frustrated or upset?
29. Do you communicate respectfully with family members?
30. Can you set goals and follow through on commitments?
31. Do you take responsibility for your mistakes?
32. Are you honest and trustworthy?
33. Do you show respect for others' property and space?
34. Can you handle criticism and feedback constructively?
35. Do you help others without expecting something in return?
36. Do you respect household rules and curfews?
